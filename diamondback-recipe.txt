Ingredients:
 - 1 hot dog shaped donut with chocolate topping
 - 1 churro
 - 2 balls of ice cream
 - Yogurt
 - Chocolate topping
 - Caramel topping

Directions:
 1. Slice horizontally, just as you would with a hot dog bun
 
 2. Put the churro between the two halfs
 
 3. Serve two balls of your favorite ice cream flavour

 4. Pour some yougurt over.

 5. Finish with the chocolate and caramel toppings
